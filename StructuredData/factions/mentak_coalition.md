# Mentak Coalition
**Expansion:** Base

## Setup
* **Commodities:** 2
* **Starting Tech:** sarween_tools, plasma_scoring
### Home Planets
* **Moll Primus**: 4 Resources / 1 Influence

---

## Faction Abilities
### Ambush
At the start of a space combat, you may roll 1 die for each of up to 2 of your cruisers or destroyers in the system. For each result equal to or greater than that ship's combat value produce 1 hit; your opponent must assign it to 1 of his ships.

### Pillage
After 1 of your neighbors gains trade goods or resolves a transaction, if he has 3 or more trade goods, you may take 1 of his trade goods or commodities.

## Leaders
### Agent: Suffi An
After the Pillage faction ability is used against another player: You may exhaust this card; if you do, you and that player each draw 1 action card.

### Commander: S'ula Mentarion
* **Unlock:** Have 4 cruisers on the game board.
After you win a space combat: You may force your opponent to give you 1 promissory note from their hand.

### Hero: Ipswitch, Loose Cannon
* **Unlock:** Have 3 scored objectives.
Sleeper Cell: At the start of a space combat that you are participating in: You may purge this card; if you do, for each other player's ship that is destroyed during this combat, place 1 ship of that type from your reinforcements in the active system.

## Units
### Corsair (Cruiser III)
* **Cost** 2 | **Combat** 6 | **Move** 3 | **Capacity** 2
* **Special Ability:** If the active system contains another player's non-fighter ships, this unit can move through systems that contain other players' ships.

### Fourth Moon
* **Cost** 8 | **Combat** 7 | **Num_of_attacks** 2 | **Move** 1 | **Capacity** 3
* **Keywords:** SUSTAIN DAMAGE
* **Special Ability:** Other players' ships in this system cannot use Sustain Damage.

### Moll Terminus
* **Cost** 2 | **Combat** 6
* **Keywords:** SUSTAIN DAMAGE
* **Special Ability:** Other players' ground forces on this planet cannot use Sustain Damage.

## Technologies & Breakthroughs
### Mirror Computing
* **Prerequisites:** Yellow, Yellow, Yellow
When you spend trade goods, each trade good is worth 2 resources or influence instead of 1.

### Salvage Operations
* **Prerequisites:** Yellow, Yellow
After you win or lose a space combat, gain 1 trade good; if you won the combat, you may also produce 1 ship in that system of any ship type that was destroyed during the combat.

### Breakthrough: The Table's Grace
* **Colors:** Yellow, Green
If you have the Cruiser II unit upgrade technology, flip this card and place it on top of Cruiser II. (Refer to Corsair for unit stats).

## Promissory Note
### Promise of Protection
ACTION: Place this card faceup in your play area. While this card is in your play area, the Mentak player cannot use his Pillage faction ability against you. If you activate a system that contains 1 or more of the Mentak player's units, return this card to the Mentak player.
