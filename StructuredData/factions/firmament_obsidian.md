# The Firmament / The Obsidian (FIRMAMENT)
**Expansion:** Thunder's Edge

## Setup
* **Commodities:** 3
* **Starting Tech:** Choose one green or yellow technology with no prerequisites.
### Home Planets
* **Cronos**: 2 Resources / 1 Influence
* **Tallin**: 1 Resources / 2 Influence

---

## Faction Abilities
### Plots within Plots
Score secret objectives already scored by others; place a facedown Plot card with that player's control token on it.

### Puppets of the Blade
ACTION: If you have at least 1 plot card, purge components to become The Obsidian.

## Leaders
### Agent: Myru Vos
Ships move through units and ignore SPACE CANNON.

### Commander: Captain Aroz
* **Unlock:** Have a plot card in play.
Treat systems with your ships as controlled for secret objectives.

### Hero: Sharsiss
ACTION: Place 1 plot with another player's token. Purge card.

### Viper Ex-23

## Technologies & Breakthroughs
### Planesplitter
* **Prerequisites:** Yellow, Yellow
When gained, put The Fracture into play.

### Neural Parasite
* **Prerequisites:** Green, Green

* **Colors:** Yellow, Green
None

## Promissory Note
### Black Ops
Holder gains 2 TG and 2 CT; Firmament player places 1 facedown Plot with holder's token. Purge card.
