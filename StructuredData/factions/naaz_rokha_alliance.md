# Naaz-Rokha Alliance
**Expansion:** Prophecy of Kings

## Setup
* **Commodities:** 3
* **Starting Tech:** psychoarchaeology, ai_development_algorithm
### Home Planets
* **Naazir**: 2 Resources / 1 Influence
* **Rokha**: 1 Resources / 2 Influence

---

## Faction Abilities
### Distant Suns
When you explore a planet that contains 1 of your mechs, you may draw 1 additional card; choose 1 to resolve and discard the rest.

### Fabrication
ACTION: Either purge 2 of your relic fragments of the same type to gain 1 relic or purge 1 of your relic fragments to gain 1 command token.

## Leaders
### Agent: Garv and Gunn
At the end of a player's turn: You may exhaust this card to allow that player to explore 1 of their planets.

### Commander: Dart and Tai
* **Unlock:** Have mechs in 3 systems.
After you gain control of a planet that was controlled by another player: You may explore that planet.

### Hero: Hesh and Prit
* **Unlock:** Have 3 scored objectives.
Perfect Synthesis: ACTION: Gain 1 relic and perform the secondary ability of up to 2 readied or unchosen strategy cards; during this action, spend command tokens from your reinforcements instead of your strategy pool. Then, purge this card.

## Units
### Eidolon
* **Cost** 2 | **Combat** 6 | **Num_of_attacks** 2
* **Keywords:** SUSTAIN DAMAGE
* **Special Ability:** If this unit is in the space area of the active system at the start of a space combat, flip this card to its Z-Grav side. This card begins the game faceup.

### Z-Grav Eidolon
* **Cost** 2 | **Combat** 8 | **Num_of_attacks** 2
* **Keywords:** SUSTAIN DAMAGE
* **Special Ability:** If this unit is in the space area of the active system, it is also a ship. At the end of a space battle in the active system, flip this card back to the ground side.

### Eidolon Maximum
* **Combat** 4 | **Num_of_attacks** 4 | **Move** 3
* **Keywords:** SUSTAIN DAMAGE
* **Special Ability:** This unit is both a ship and ground force. It cannot be assigned hits from unit abilities. Repair it at the start of every combat round. Game effects cannot place or produce your mechs. When destroyed or removed, return the breakthrough to your play area.

### Visz El Vir
* **Cost** 8 | **Combat** 9 | **Num_of_attacks** 2 | **Move** 1 | **Capacity** 4
* **Keywords:** SUSTAIN DAMAGE
* **Special Ability:** Your mechs in this system roll 1 additional die during combat.

## Technologies & Breakthroughs
### Pre-Fab Arcologies
* **Prerequisites:** Green, Green, Green
After you explore a planet, ready that planet.

### Supercharge
* **Prerequisites:** Red
At the start of a combat round, you may exhaust this card to apply +1 to the result of each of your unit's combat rolls during this combat round.

### Breakthrough: Absolute Synergy
* **Colors:** Blue, Green
When you have 4 mechs in the same system, you may return 3 of those mechs to your reinforcements to flip this card and place it on top of your mech card. (Refer to Eidolon Maximum for unit stats).

## Promissory Note
### Black Market Forgery
ACTION: Purge 2 of your relic fragments of the same type to gain 1 relic. Then, return this card to the Naaz-Rokha player.
