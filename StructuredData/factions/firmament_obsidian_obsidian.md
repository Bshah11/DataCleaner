# The Firmament / The Obsidian (OBSIDIAN)
**Expansion:** Thunder's Edge

## Setup
* **Commodities:** 3
* **Starting Tech:** Choose one green or yellow technology with no prerequisites.
### Home Planets
* **Cronos Hollow**: 3 Resources / 0 Influence
* **Tallin Hollow**: 3 Resources / 0 Influence

---

## Faction Abilities
### The Blade's Orchestra
When entering play, flip home system and plot cards; ready 'Hollow' planets if controlled.

### Marionettes
The players whose tokens are on Plot cards are 'puppeted players' for The Obsidian.

## Leaders
### Agent: Vos Hollow
When a ship is destroyed, opponent must destroy ship of same type.

### Commander: Aroz Hollow
* **Unlock:** Have units in The Fracture
+1 to combat rolls in The Fracture.

### Hero: Sharsiss Hollow
ACTION: Ready all of your planets. Purge card.

* **Keywords:** SUSTAIN DAMAGE
* **Special Ability:** None

### Viper Ex-23
* **Cost** 2 | **Combat** 6
* **Keywords:** SUSTAIN DAMAGE

## Technologies & Breakthroughs
### Planesplitter
* **Prerequisites:** None
This technology cannot be researched; it carries over from Firmament.

### Neural Parasite
* **Prerequisites:** None
Start of turn: destroy 1 adjacent enemy infantry.

### Breakthrough: The Sowing / The Reaping
* **Colors:** Yellow, Green
None

## Promissory Note
### Malevolency
At status end, non-Obsidian holder removes 1 fleet token. ACTION: Give this to a neighbor.

## Plot Deck
### Assail
+1 to combat/unit ability rolls against puppeted player.

### Enervate
Resolve puppeted player's secondary strategy abilities for free (Leadership primary instead).

### Extract
Reveal: Gain 1 non-faction tech from puppeted player.

### Seethe
Reveal: Destroy units on a puppeted non-home planet.

### Siphon
When puppeted player gains commodities, you gain trade goods.
