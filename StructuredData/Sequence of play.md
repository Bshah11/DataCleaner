# Twilight Imperium 4th Edition: Sequence of Play

## I. Strategy Phase
Beginning with the Speaker and moving clockwise, each player chooses one **Strategy Card** from the common play area.
* **3 or 4 Player Game:** Each player chooses two Strategy Cards.
* **5 to 8 Player Game:** Each player chooses one Strategy Card.

---

## II. Action Phase
In order of **Initiative** (the number on the Strategy Card), players take turns. This continues until all players have passed. On a turn, a player may perform one of the following:

### 1. Tactical Action
The most common action, used to move, fight, and produce. (See "Tactical Action Steps" for detailed sub-sequence).

### 2. Strategic Action
The player resolves the **Primary Ability** on their Strategy Card. All other players may then resolve the **Secondary Ability** of that card.

### 3. Component Action
The player resolves an "ACTION" ability on one of their components (Action Card, Technology, Leader, or Faction Sheet).

---

## III. Status Phase
After all players have passed, resolve the following steps in order:

1. **Score Objectives:** In initiative order, each player may score 1 Public and 1 Secret objective.
2. **Reveal Public Objective:** The Speaker reveals the next objective from the deck.
3. **Draw Action Cards:** Each player draws 1 Action Card.
3. **Remove Command Tokens:** Each player will remove all the command tokens from the game board 
4. **Gain and Redistribute Tokens:** * Each player gains 2 command tokens from their reinforcements.
    * Each player may redistribute all their command tokens among their three pools (Tactical, Fleet, and Strategy).
5. **Ready Cards:** Players ready all exhausted cards (Planets, Technology, etc.).
6. **Repair Units:** All units with **SUSTAIN DAMAGE** flip back to their undamaged side.
7. **Return Strategy Cards:** All Strategy Cards are returned to the common play area.

---

## IV. Agenda Phase
This phase occurs only after Mecatol Rex has been claimed.

1. **First Agenda:** * The Speaker reveals the top card of the Agenda deck.
    * **Voting:** Players cast votes (Influence) to resolve the agenda.
2. **Second Agenda:** * The Speaker reveals the next card of the Agenda deck.
    * **Voting:** Players cast votes to resolve the agenda.
3. **Ready Planets:** Players ready all planets that were exhausted to cast votes.

---

# Detailed Sub-Sequences

## Tactical Action Steps
When a player performs a Tactical Action, they must resolve these steps in order:

1. **Activation:** Place a command token from the Tactical Pool in a system.
2. **Movement:** Move ships into the active system. Units may transport ground forces and fighters.
3. **Space Combat:** If two players have ships in the active system:
    * Anti-Fighter Barrage
    * Announce Retreat
    * Roll Dice
    * Assign Hits
