## Assimilate
* **Component ID:** the_l1z1x_mindnet_assimilate
* **Component Type:** Faction Ability

### Clarification Notes
1. Any space docks that are placed by this ability during a tactical action may produce units during theProductionstep of that tactical action.
2. If the L1Z1X player resolves theirAssimilateability, and then plays theInfiltrateaction card, or vice versa, they will replace zero PDS and space docks with zero PDS and space docks when they resolve the latter ability.
3. If the L1Z1X player would have to place a structure, but there are none of that type left in their reinforcements, they may remove a structure of that type from any system that does not contain one of their command tokens and place that instead.
4. The L1Z1X player must mandatorily replace every structure. However, if there are none of that type in their reinforcements they are not forced to do so.

## Harrow
* **Component ID:** the_l1z1x_mindnet_harrow
* **Component Type:** Faction Ability

### Clarification Notes
1. ThePlanetary Shieldability will prevent the use of theHarrowability on a planet.
2. The L1Z1X player cannot use theirHarrowability if they are not the active player. This will most commonly occur due to the effect of the N’orr player’s commander, G’hom Sek’kus.
3. The L1Z1X player may use theirHarrowability at the end of the round in which their last ground force was destroyed. This may cause the combat to end in a draw.
4. During theBombardmentstep, each of the L1Z1X player’s units with theBombardmentability is assigned only one planet to bombard. However, during ground combat, every unit in the system with theBombardmentability may use it against the planet the ground combat is taking place on.
5. An Annihilator may bombard when usingHarrowif that mech is not currently participating in ground combat, even if it has been committed to a different planet, and has been or will be involved in another ground combat during the current invasion.
6. The L1Z1X player may use the ability of theX–89 Bacterial Weapon ΩorX–89 Bacterial Weapon ΩΩtechnologies whenever they bombard.

## The Helmsman — Dark Space Navigation
* **Component ID:** the_l1z1x_mindnet_the_helmsman_dark_space_navigation
* **Component Type:** Hero

### Clarification Notes
1. Ships in systems with one of the L1Z1X player’s command tokens may move.
2. Ships may transport units only if their origin system does not contain one of the L1Z1X player’s command tokens.
3. A future rule change will allow a player’s ships to transport units from systems that contain that player’s command token when those ships move outside of a tactical action.
4. Ships move directly to their destination system. They may only transport units from their origin system.
5. Ships moving out of a gravity rift must roll for removal.
6. Ships cannot move into a nebula or supernova, even if it contains one of the L1Z1X player’s command tokens.
7. Ships may travel into an asteroid field only if the L1Z1X player owns theAntimass Deflectorstechnology.
8. Dark Space Navigationmay be used to move ships into the wormhole nexus. If the nexus is inactive, this will cause it to activate.
