## War Funding
* **Component ID:** the_barony_of_letnev_war_funding
* **Component Type:** Promissory Note

### Clarification Notes
1. A player may playWar Fundingwhen the Letnev player has zero or one trade goods. The Letnev player will lose the single trade good, if applicable, and the other effects will resolve as normal.
2. A player may receiveWar Fundingin a transaction during a combat. If received at the start of a round, that player may play it immediately.
3. A transaction must involve the active player. As such, the defender will be unable to receiveWar Funding.
4. The active player may perform a transaction with each other player at most once per turn. As such, the active player cannot receiveWar Fundingmore than once per turn.
5. If the active player receivedWar Fundingon a previous turn, they may play it and immediately perform a transaction with the Letnev player to regain it. However,War Fundingcannot be played twice in one timing window. The active player cannot playWar Fundingagain until the next round of combat.
6. If the attacker receivesWar Fundingafter the defender has played it, the attacker cannot play it during the same round of combat.

## Non–Euclidean Shielding
* **Component ID:** the_barony_of_letnev_noneuclidean_shielding
* **Component Type:** Technology

### Clarification Notes
1. Non–Euclidean Shieldingmay be applied when a Dunlain Reaper uses itsSustain damageability.
2. Non–Euclidean Shieldingapplies whenever hits are produced. This includes hits produced byBombardmentandSpace Cannonabilities.
3. If there are multiple hits that can be cancelled, then the Letnev player must cancel two hits whenever one of their units uses itsSustain damageability; they cannot cancel just one.
4. Say the Letnev player has exactly one hit produced against them remaining, and one of their units uses itsSustain damageability to cancel it. If additional hits are produced against the Letnev player's units, such as with theReflective Shieldingaction card, the Letnev player cannot retroactively use the previous use ofSustain damageto allowNon–Euclidean Shieldingto cancel a second hit.

## Rear Admiral Farran
* **Component ID:** the_barony_of_letnev_rear_admiral_farran
* **Component Type:** Commander

### Clarification Notes
1. If the Letnev player has more ships in a system than allowed for by their fleet pool plusArmada, they must first remove ships to meet this limit before they can unlock Rear Admiral Farran.
2. This effect may be applied when a Dunlain Reaper uses itsSustain damageability.
3. If the Ul player has the Letnev player’sAlliancepromissory note, then this effect may be applied when a Hel–Titan PDS uses itsSustain damageability.
4. A unit that becomes damaged without using itsSustain Damageability will not trigger this effect.
5. If multiple units use theirSustain Damageability within a single step, the trade goods are gained one at a time.

## Dunlain Reaper
* **Component ID:** the_barony_of_letnev_dunlain_reaper
* **Component Type:** Mech

### Clarification Notes
1. ADeployability may be resolved only once per timing window. As such, the Letnev player may only place one Dunlain Reaper this way per ground combat round.
2. Further Dunlain Reapers may be placed in later rounds.
3. ADeployability may only be resolved for units in a player’s reinforcements. If all four Dunlain Reapers are already on the board, no Dunlain Reaper may be deployed.
4. If a Dunlain Reaper is destroyed during combat, then another may be deployed in a later round of the same combat.

## Gravleash Maneuvers
* **Component ID:** the_barony_of_letnev_gravleash_maneuvers
* **Component Type:** Breakthrough

### Clarification Notes
1. If a ship has its movement value increased by an ability such as theGravity Drivetechnology, then other ships will have their movement values increased to match the modified value.
2. If the Letnev player owns the Fighter II unit upgrade technology, another ship may have its move value increased to match that of a Fighter II in that system.
