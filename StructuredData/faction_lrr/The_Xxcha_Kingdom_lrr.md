## Instinct Training
* **Component ID:** the_xxcha_kingdom_instinct_training
* **Component Type:** Technology

### Clarification Notes
1. Instinct Trainingmay cancel aSabotageaction card.
2. If a player has a second copy of an action card, they may play it if the first was cancelled byInstinct Training.
3. If an action card is cancelled byInstinct Training, no costs are paid.
4. A player playing an action card announces the following beforeInstinct Trainingmay be played:
5. Any targeted planets, system, units, players, cards, outcomes, and/or other such objects.
6. The value of any variable cost to be paid.
7. Any dice are rolled and any cards are drawn only after all other players has declined to playInstinct Training.
8. If an action card instructs a player to research a technology, that player need not announce what technology they are researching until all other players has declined to playInstinct Training.
9. If an action card is played that will have an effect later, such as a rider, that card may only be cancelled byInstinct Trainingwhen it is originally played.
10. If the action card that was cancelled byInstinct Trainingwas to be used to perform a component action, the active player must perform a different action, or pass.
11. If the component action was to be the second action the active player was to perform on their turn, through an effect such as theFleet Logisticstechnology, the active player may end their turn without performing a second action and without passing.

## Xxekir Grom — Political Data Nexus
* **Component ID:** the_xxcha_kingdom_xxekir_grom_political_data_nexus
* **Component Type:** Hero

### Clarification Notes
1. If the Xxcha player draws one of theClassified Document Leaks,Judicial Abolishment,Miscount DisclosedorNew Constitutionagendas, they will only discard it (if required) and reveal a new one if they choose to reveal that agenda.
2. If the Xxcha player draws theCovert Legislationagenda, they will only draw a hidden agenda if they choose to reveal it. If the Xxcha player is not the speaker, the speaker will view the hidden agenda, but the Xxcha player will not see it until it is either discarded fromQuash, or resolved.
3. If the Xxcha player draws, reveals and resolves theMiscount Disclosedagenda, they cast one vote for an outcome of the elected law, and resolve it.
4. If the Xxcha player draws, reveals and resolves thePublic Executionagenda, the elected player may still vote in the agenda phase following the current action phase.
5. When the Xxcha player chooses and reveals an agenda, they may then use theirQuashability.
6. After the Xxcha player chooses and reveals an agenda, they may play any rider action cards on the revealed agenda.
7. The Xxcha player chooses the order the two agendas are resolved.
8. The two agendas are revealed before either is resolved.
9. The Xxcha player will resolve all abilities that trigger when or after an agenda is revealed for the first agenda before revealing the second agenda.
10. When an agenda outcome with an immediate effect resolves, all players resolve the effect.
11. If an agenda would give multiple players a victory point, those players gain that victory point in initiative order, regardless of the Xxcha player’s initiative.
12. Other players may still perform one transaction total with the Xxcha player during this turn, if they are neighbors.
13. If theRepresentative Governmentlaw is in play, it will have no effect on the Xxcha player as they resolvePolitical Data Nexus.
14. If a third law is put into play, theDictate Policyobjective cannot be scored until the agenda phase.
15. TheDrive the Debateobjective cannot be scored while resolvingPolitical Data Nexus.
16. Effects from agenda outcomes that occur “at the end of this agenda phase” are ignored.
17. Effects from agenda outcomes that occur “at the start of the next strategy phase” will still occur.

## Xxekir Grom — Political Data Nexus
* **Component ID:** the_xxcha_kingdom_xxekir_grom_political_data_nexus
* **Component Type:** Hero
* **Revision ID:** the_xxcha_kingdom_xxekir_grom_political_data_nexus_1
* **Source:** Codex
* **Revision Number:** 1
* **Status:** Current

### Clarification Notes
1. During the status phase, public and secret objectives are scored simultaneously. The Xxcha player cannot score a public objective, unlock Xxekir Grom, and then usePolitical Data Nexus Ωin order to score a secret objective, or vice versa.
2. The combined value may be spent as either influence or resources, not both, and cannot be split between the two.
3. If the Xxcha player owns a planet with three influence and three resources, they cannot score theAmass Wealthobjective by exhausting just it and spending three trade goods.
4. Political Data Nexus Ωonly changes the spendable influence and resources. It does not change the value for any other game effect.
5. A space dock will not add the influence value of the planet it is on to itsProductionvalue.
6. The influence value of a planet will not increase the number of units the Xxcha player may produce when using theIntegrated Economytechnology.
7. The resource values of the planets the Xxcha player controls will not contribute to unlocking Elder Qanoj.
8. Political Data Nexus Ωdoes not apply when scoring theEstablish HegemonyorHoard Raw Materialsobjectives.
9. The Xxcha player will not add the influence value of a planet when they gain trade goods with theMining Initiativeaction card. Other players will not add the influence value when they gain trade goods from theUprisingaction card if they choose to exhaust one of the Xxcha player’s planets.
10. The resource value of a planet will be added to its influence value when the Xxcha player casts votes during the agenda phase.

## Archon’s Gift
* **Component ID:** the_xxcha_kingdom_archons_gift
* **Component Type:** Breakthrough

### Clarification Notes
1. A source of both influence and resources, such as a planet, may be spent for one or the other, but not both.
2. For example, Archon Tau will provide one resource or one influence, not two.
3. A source of both influence or resources, such as a planet, may be spent as one or the other, but cannot be split to be spent as both.
4. For example, if the Xxcha player wishes to score theAmass Wealthobjective, each planet they exhaust will contribute to either the resource requirement or the influence requirement, but cannot partially contribute to both.
5. Archon’s Giftonly changes how a planet’s influence and resources may be spent. It does not change the value for any other game effect.
6. A space dock cannot use the influence value of the planet it is on to calculate itsProductionvalue.
7. The influence value of a planet will not increase the number of units the Xxcha player may produce when using theIntegrated Economytechnology.
8. The resource values of the planets the Xxcha player controls will not contribute to unlocking Elder Qanoj.
9. Archon’s Giftdoes not apply when scoring theEstablish HegemonyorHoard Raw Materialsobjectives.
10. The Xxcha player cannot use the influence value of a planet when they gain trade goods with theMining Initiativeaction card. Other players cannot use the influence value when they gain trade goods from theUprisingaction card if they choose to exhaust one of the Xxcha player’s planets.
