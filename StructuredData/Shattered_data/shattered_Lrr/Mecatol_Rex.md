# Mecatol Rex
## Specific rule - [Mecatol Rex is the planet placed in the center of the game board during setup.]
### Rule points
1. During setup, the custodians token is placed on Mecatol Rex. This token prevents a player from committing ground forces to land on the planet unless they spend six influence to remove the token.

### Notes
- Mecatol Rex on tile 18 is not a legendary planet. Mecatol Rex on tile 112 is a legendary planet.
