# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Nomad
## TYPE: Agent
### COMPONENT: Artuno the Betrayer
#### ID: the_nomad_artuno_the_betrayer

1. If the Nomad player would gain trade goods from multiple effects, they may only put the trade goods from one of those effects on Artuno the Betrayer.
2. For example, if the Nomad player successfully predicts the outcome of an agenda via theTrade Rideraction card, they could put either the trade goods gained from theTrade Rider, or fromFuture Sight, but not both.
3. When a player gains trade goods that were on a strategy card, they do not gain them from the supply.
4. When the Nomad player gains trade goods that were on Artuno the Betrayer, they do not gain them from the supply.
