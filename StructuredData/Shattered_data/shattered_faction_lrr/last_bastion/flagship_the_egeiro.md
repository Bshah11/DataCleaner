# LRR CLARIFICATION: Last Bastion
## TYPE: Flagship
### COMPONENT: The Egeiro
#### ID: last_bastion_the_egeiro

1. If the Bastion player controls multiple planets in a system,The Egeirowill apply +1 to its combat rolls just once.
2. If the Bastion player controls a planet in a system, and other players control other planets in that system,The Egeirowill still apply +1 to its combat rolls from that system.
