# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Mentak Coalition
## TYPE: Commander
### COMPONENT: S’ula Mentarion
#### ID: the_mentak_coalition_sula_mentarion

1. The Mentak player’s opponent chooses which promissory note they give to the Mentak player.
2. Giving a promissory note because of S’ula Mentarion is not resolving a transaction.
3. If the Mentak player resolves theirSalvage Operationsto place their fourth cruiser faction technology after winning a space combat, they cannot use the ability of S’ula Mentarion from winning that combat.
4. If the Mentak player places their fourth cruiser during combat, such as by the effect ofSleeper Cell, they may unlock and use the ability of S’ula Mentarion at the end of that combat, should they win.
5. The four cruisers need not survive until the end of combat; S’ula Mentarion is unlocked from the placement of the fourth cruiser on the game board.
