# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Mentak Coalition
## TYPE: Unit
### COMPONENT: Corsair
#### ID: the_mentak_coalition_corsair

1. If the active system contains a non–Mentak player’s units, the Mentak player may move a Corsair through systems containing any other player’s ships, not just those of the player in the active system.
2. The ability of the Corsair may be triggered even if the Mentak player has some of their own units in the active system.
3. The Nekro player cannot place a Valefar token on the Corsair card.
