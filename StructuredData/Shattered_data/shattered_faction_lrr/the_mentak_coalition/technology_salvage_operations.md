# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Mentak Coalition
## TYPE: Technology
### COMPONENT: Salvage Operations
#### ID: the_mentak_coalition_salvage_operations

1. The produced unit must be paid for.
2. If the space combat ends in a draw,Salvage Operationshas no effect.
3. If the Mentak player wishes to place a unit using theirSalvage Operationsability, but there are none of that type left in their reinforcements, they can remove a unit of that type from any system that does not contain one of their command tokens and place that instead.
4. If the opponent has a faction specific ship, including their flagship, destroyed, the Mentak player may use theirSalvage Operationsto produce a generic ship of the same type, or theirFourth Moon, as appropriate.
5. The Mentak player cannot produce ground forces usingSalvage Operations, even if their opponent has the ability to treat a ground force as a ship.
6. The Mentak player cannot produce a war sun unless they have their war sun technology.
