# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Winnu
## TYPE: Faction Ability
### COMPONENT: Reclamation
#### ID: the_winnu_reclamation

1. Placing the structure will occur after theProductionstep. If the Winnu player places a Space Dock, they cannot produce out of it during the same action.
2. If the Winnu player has no PDS or Space Docks left in their reinforcements, they may remove a unit from any system that does not contain one of their command tokens and place that unit on Mecatol Rex when resolvingReclamation.
