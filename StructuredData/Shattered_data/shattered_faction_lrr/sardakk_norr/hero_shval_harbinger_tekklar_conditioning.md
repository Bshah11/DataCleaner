# LRR CLARIFICATION: Sardakk N’orr
## TYPE: Hero
### COMPONENT: Sh’val, Harbinger — Tekklar Conditioning
#### ID: sardakk_norr_shval_harbinger_tekklar_conditioning

1. TheParleyaction card will place the committed ground forces in the space area of the active system. As the N’orr player will not have enough capacity in the space area for their ground forces, they will have to remove all ground forces from that planet, that are now in the space area of that system.
2. TheSpace Cannon Offense,Space CombatandBombardmentsteps are skipped.
3. Another player may still produce hits against the N’orr player’s ground forces committed usingTekklar Conditioningduring theSpace Cannon Defensestep.
4. Any abilities that trigger “after a player moves ships…” or similar cannot be used followingTekklar Conditioning.
