# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Vuil’raith Cabal
## TYPE: Technology
### COMPONENT: Vortex
#### ID: the_vuilraith_cabal_vortex

1. A unit is not adjacent to the system it is in; theVortexability cannot target a ground force on another planet in the same systems as one of the Vuil’raith player’s Dimensional Tears, unless there is a second space dock in an adjacent system.
2. Vortexcannot be used if there are no units that can be targeted.
3. If a player has all their units of a particular type on the board, other than fighters or infantry, that type of unit belonging to that player cannot be targeted.
4. If another player is blockading a Dimensional Tear, their units cannot be targeted withVortex.
