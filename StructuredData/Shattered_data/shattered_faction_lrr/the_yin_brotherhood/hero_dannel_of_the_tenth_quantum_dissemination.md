# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Yin Brotherhood
## TYPE: Hero
### COMPONENT: Dannel of the Tenth  — Quantum Dissemination
#### ID: the_yin_brotherhood_dannel_of_the_tenth__quantum_dissemination

1. A total of three infantry may be placed. These may all be placed on one planet, or placed one each on three planets, or split two/one between two planets.
2. The Yin player may commit infantry to Mallice even if the wormhole nexus has not been flipped to its active side. Doing so will cause the wormhole nexus to flip.
3. Only theCommit Ground Forces,Ground CombatandEstablish Controlsteps of an invasion are resolved.
4. The Yin player commits all infantry before resolving any combats.
5. Only the three infantry from the Yin player’s reinforcements may be committed during theCommit Ground Forcesstep. Ground forces in the space area above the planets cannot be committed.
6. The Yin player may use theirIndoctrinationability and their mech’sDeployability.
7. The Yin player does not activate the systems containing the planets that they are committing infantry to. As such, any effects that trigger “when/after a player activates a system” do not trigger.
8. TheParleyaction card may be used on one planet when the Yin player resolvesQuantum Dissemination.
9. If the Yin player has no ships with capacity in the planet’s system, then the infantry will be removed from the board.
10. If the Yin player does have ships with capacity in the planet’s system, then the infantry will be placed in the space area of the system. The Yin player might then need to remove infantry and/or fighters from the system to satisfy the capacity limit.
