# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Yin Brotherhood
## TYPE: Mech
### COMPONENT: Moyin’s Ashes
#### ID: the_yin_brotherhood_moyins_ashes

1. ADeployability may only be resolved for units in a player’s reinforcements. If all four Moyin’s Ashes are already on the board, no more may be deployed.
2. If a Moyin’s Ashes is destroyed during combat, another may be deployed in a later combat during the same invasion.
3. The removed unit must still be an infantry.
4. TheDeployability has a total cost of three influence. The Yin player may exhaust a single planet with three or more influence to pay this cost.
