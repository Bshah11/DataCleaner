# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Yin Brotherhood
## TYPE: Technology
### COMPONENT: Impulse Core
#### ID: the_yin_brotherhood_impulse_core

1. When resolvingImpulse Core, the Yin player’s opponent chooses which ship is assigned the hit.
2. Effects that cancel hits, such as theSustain Damageability, may be used to cancel a hit produced byImpulse Core.
3. Impulse Corewill resolve before theAnti–Fighter Barragestep of a space combat. As such, any destroyed destroyers will not make an anti–fighter barrage roll.
