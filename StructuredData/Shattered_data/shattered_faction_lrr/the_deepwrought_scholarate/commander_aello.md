# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Deepwrought Scholarate
## TYPE: Commander
### COMPONENT: Aello
#### ID: the_deepwrought_scholarate_aello

1. The Deepwrought player does not have the capability to prevent other players from using the ability of Aello.
2. Aello cannot be used reduce a cost paid with trade goods, influence, or any other currency.
3. If a player has the Deepwrought player’sAlliancepromissory note, then other players may use the ability of either or both (or neither):
4. Another player may use the ability from both for a two resource discount, triggering the commodity ability for both the Deepwrought player and the player with theirAlliancepromissory note.
5. Another player may use the ability from only the Deepwrought player for a one resource discount, triggering the commodity ability for just the Deepwrought player.
6. Another player may use the ability from only the player with theAlliancepromissory note for a one resource discount, triggering the commodity ability for just the player with theAlliancepromissory note.
7. Another player may use the ability from neither player, and receive no discount; the commodity ability will trigger for neither the Deepwrought player nor the player with theirAlliancepromissory note.
8. The Deepwrought player and the player with theirAlliancepromissory note may each use the ability of the other for a one resource discount, triggering the commodity ability for that other player.
9. If the Mahact player has the Deepwrought player’s command token in their fleet pool, then this gives each other player the option of another discount. The Deepwrought player, the player with theirAlliancepromissory note, and the Mahact player may choose up to two of the other players with the ability, triggering their commodity ability, and resulting in a corresponding discount of up to two resources. All other players may choose up to three of the players with the ability, triggering their commodity ability, and resulting in a corresponding discount of up to three resources.
