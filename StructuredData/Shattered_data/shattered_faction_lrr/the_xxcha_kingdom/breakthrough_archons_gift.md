# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Xxcha Kingdom
## TYPE: Breakthrough
### COMPONENT: Archon’s Gift
#### ID: the_xxcha_kingdom_archons_gift

1. A source of both influence and resources, such as a planet, may be spent for one or the other, but not both.
2. For example, Archon Tau will provide one resource or one influence, not two.
3. A source of both influence or resources, such as a planet, may be spent as one or the other, but cannot be split to be spent as both.
4. For example, if the Xxcha player wishes to score theAmass Wealthobjective, each planet they exhaust will contribute to either the resource requirement or the influence requirement, but cannot partially contribute to both.
5. Archon’s Giftonly changes how a planet’s influence and resources may be spent. It does not change the value for any other game effect.
6. A space dock cannot use the influence value of the planet it is on to calculate itsProductionvalue.
7. The influence value of a planet will not increase the number of units the Xxcha player may produce when using theIntegrated Economytechnology.
8. The resource values of the planets the Xxcha player controls will not contribute to unlocking Elder Qanoj.
9. Archon’s Giftdoes not apply when scoring theEstablish HegemonyorHoard Raw Materialsobjectives.
10. The Xxcha player cannot use the influence value of a planet when they gain trade goods with theMining Initiativeaction card. Other players cannot use the influence value when they gain trade goods from theUprisingaction card if they choose to exhaust one of the Xxcha player’s planets.
