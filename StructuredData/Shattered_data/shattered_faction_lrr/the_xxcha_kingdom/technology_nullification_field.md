# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Xxcha Kingdom
## TYPE: Technology
### COMPONENT: Nullification Field
#### ID: the_xxcha_kingdom_nullification_field

1. Once the Xxcha player resolves the ability ofNullification Field, the active player cannot use any “after you activate a system” abilities.
2. Other players, including the Xxcha player, cannot use any “after another player activates a system that…” abilities.
3. The active player may still use any “at the end of your turn” abilities.
