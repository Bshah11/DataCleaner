# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Barony of Letnev
## TYPE: Breakthrough
### COMPONENT: Gravleash Maneuvers
#### ID: the_barony_of_letnev_gravleash_maneuvers

1. If a ship has its movement value increased by an ability such as theGravity Drivetechnology, then other ships will have their movement values increased to match the modified value.
2. If the Letnev player owns the Fighter II unit upgrade technology, another ship may have its move value increased to match that of a Fighter II in that system.
