# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Mahact Gene–Sorcerers
## TYPE: Hero
### COMPONENT: Airo Shir Aur — Benediction
#### ID: the_mahact_genesorcerers_airo_shir_aur_benediction

1. Fighters and ground forces in the space area of the origin system are moved. Ground forces on planets are left behind.
2. If the Mahact player moves their own ships, they may transport ground forces on planets.
3. If the Mahact player moves the Saar player’s units, any Floating Factories in the origin system will also be moved.
4. Any abilities that trigger when a player moves ships trigger from the perspective of the Mahact player.
5. Any abilities that trigger when a player’s ships move trigger from the perspective of the player that owns them.
6. Any abilities that trigger when a player moves their ships do not trigger.
7. The Mahact player may move another player’s ships into an asteroid field only if that player owns theAntimass Deflectorstechnology.
8. The Mahact player may move another player’s ships into a supernova only if that player owns theMagmus ReactorMuaat factional technology.
9. If the Mahact player moves a player’s ships out of a gravity rift, that player must roll for removal.
10. If the Mahact player is moving the Vuil’raith player’s ships out of a system containing a Dimensional Tear, the Vuil’raith player will not roll for removal.
11. The Mahact player cannot move ships through a wormhole while theEnforced Travel Banlaw is in play.
12. The Mahact player cannot move the Creuss player’s ships from a system with an alpha wormhole into a system with a beta wormhole (and no alpha wormhole), or vice versa.
13. If the Mahact player moves the Muaat player’s war sun, the Muaat player cannot trigger theirNova Seedhero ability.
14. The system the combat is taking place in is considered the active system during that combat.
15. The player whose ships were moved is considered to be the attacker, the other player is considered to be the defender.
16. Regardless of which of the two players in combat is the attacker and which is the defender, the next player in initiative order will resolve abilities first, after the Mahact player.
17. ResolvingBenedictionis not a tactical action. TheActivation,Movement,InvasionandProductionsteps are not resolved.
18. Any abilities that occur “when/after you/another player activate a system” will not trigger.
19. TheSpace Cannon Offensesubstep is not resolved.
20. Either player in the combat may score theBrave the Void,Darken the Skies,Demonstrate Your Power,Destroy their Greatest Ship,Fight with Precision,Spark a Rebellion, orUnveil Flagshipsecret objectives.
21. The Mahact player cannot score these secret objectives unless they are participating in the combat.
22. No player may score theBetray a Friendsecret objective.
