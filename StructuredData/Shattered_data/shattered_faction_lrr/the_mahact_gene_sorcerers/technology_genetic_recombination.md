# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Mahact Gene–Sorcerers
## TYPE: Technology
### COMPONENT: Genetic Recombination
#### ID: the_mahact_genesorcerers_genetic_recombination

1. A player that cannot vote due to a game effect or because they have zero influence on readied planets cannot be targeted byGenetic Recombination.
2. If a player then gains the ability to vote, such as by gaining the Xxcha player’Alliancepromissory note, they may then be targeted byGenetic Recombination.
