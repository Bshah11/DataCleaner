# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Mahact Gene–Sorcerers
## TYPE: Mech
### COMPONENT: Starlancer
#### ID: the_mahact_genesorcerers_starlancer

1. Once the Mahact player resolves the ability of a Starlancer, the active player cannot use any “after you activate a system” abilities.
2. Other players, including the Mahact player, cannot use any “after another player activates a system that…” abilities.
3. The active player may still use any “at the end of your turn” abilities.
4. The Mahact player may use the ability to end their own turn and gain their own spent command token from their fleet pool.
5. The Starlancer must be in the system before activation to be able to use its ability.
