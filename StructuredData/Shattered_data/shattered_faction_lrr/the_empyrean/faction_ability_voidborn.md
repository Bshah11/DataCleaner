# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Empyrean
## TYPE: Faction Ability
### COMPONENT: Voidborn
#### ID: the_empyrean_voidborn

1. This effect also applies outside theMovementstep.
2. The Empyrean player treats nebulae as nebulae and as anomalies for all other game purposes.
3. When the Empyrean player is the defender during a space combat in a nebula, they will receive the +1 to their combat rolls.
