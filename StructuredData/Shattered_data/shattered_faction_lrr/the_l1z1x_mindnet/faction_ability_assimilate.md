# LRR CLARIFICATION: The L1Z1X Mindnet
## TYPE: Faction Ability
### COMPONENT: Assimilate
#### ID: the_l1z1x_mindnet_assimilate

1. Any space docks that are placed by this ability during a tactical action may produce units during theProductionstep of that tactical action.
2. If the L1Z1X player resolves theirAssimilateability, and then plays theInfiltrateaction card, or vice versa, they will replace zero PDS and space docks with zero PDS and space docks when they resolve the latter ability.
3. If the L1Z1X player would have to place a structure, but there are none of that type left in their reinforcements, they may remove a structure of that type from any system that does not contain one of their command tokens and place that instead.
4. The L1Z1X player must mandatorily replace every structure. However, if there are none of that type in their reinforcements they are not forced to do so.
