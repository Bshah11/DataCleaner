# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Arborec
## TYPE: Mech
### COMPONENT: Letani Behemoth
#### ID: the_arborec_letani_behemoth

1. The Arborec player may produce Letani Behemoth with their space docks.
2. The Arborec player cannot use the secondary ability of the Warfare strategy card to use theProductionability of a Letani Behemoth.
