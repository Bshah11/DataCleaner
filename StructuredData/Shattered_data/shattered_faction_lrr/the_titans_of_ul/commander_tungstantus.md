# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Titans of Ul
## TYPE: Commander
### COMPONENT: Tungstantus
#### ID: the_titans_of_ul_tungstantus

1. The trade good may be spent on the units produced by theProductionability that triggered Tungstantus.
2. The trade good may be spent as it is gained. The number of trade goods the Ul player has after producing units is used to determine if the Mentak player may use theirPillageability.
3. A trade good will not be gained unless at least one unit is produced.
4. Other effects that produce units without a unit using itsProductionability will not trigger Tungstantus.
