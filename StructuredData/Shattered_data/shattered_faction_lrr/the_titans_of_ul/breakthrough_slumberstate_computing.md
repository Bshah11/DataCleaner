# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Titans of Ul
## TYPE: Breakthrough
### COMPONENT: Slumberstate Computing
#### ID: the_titans_of_ul_slumberstate_computing

1. The Ul player does not have to be neighbors with the player that agrees to have a sleeper token placed on their planet.
2. Agreeing to have a sleeper token placed is not in and of itself a transaction, though the deal may involve a transaction.
