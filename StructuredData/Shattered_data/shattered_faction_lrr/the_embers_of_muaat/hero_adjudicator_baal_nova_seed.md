# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Embers of Muaat
## TYPE: Hero
### COMPONENT: Adjudicator Ba’al — Nova Seed
#### ID: the_embers_of_muaat_adjudicator_baal_nova_seed

1. Any method of movement is sufficient to trigger this ability, not only tactical action movement. For example, the Muaat player may triggerNova Seedwhen they retreat.
2. If the Mahact player moves the Muaat player’s war sun via the use of theirBenedictionhero ability, the Muaat player cannot triggerNova Seed.
3. If the war sun is in the target system when it is activated, it must move out of the system, and back in, forNova Seedto be able to be triggered.
4. Any command tokens, frontier tokens and the Muaat player’s units on the replaced system tile are transferred to the new supernova tile. Any Creuss wormhole tokens are returned to the Creuss player and any Ul sleeper tokens are returned to the Ul player. All other tokens are purged.
5. The Muaat player’s units may exist in and move out of the supernova system, even if the Muaat player does not ownMagmus Reactor.
6. If the Muaat player ownsMagmus Reactor Ω, and resolves this ability during a tactical action, they will be able to produce units during theProductionstep, if the Muaat player still has units in the system.
7. Nova Seedcannot target the home system of an eliminated player.
8. Nova Seedmay target the Creuss Gate (tile 17) or the Wormhole Nexus (tile 82).
9. The Empyrean player cannot use the ability of their agent, Acamar, on the Muaat player after they useNova Seedif the original system tile contained planets.
