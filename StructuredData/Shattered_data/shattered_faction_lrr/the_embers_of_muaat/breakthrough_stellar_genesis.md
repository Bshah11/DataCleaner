# LRR CLARIFICATION: The Embers of Muaat
## TYPE: Breakthrough
### COMPONENT: Stellar Genesis
#### ID: the_embers_of_muaat_stellar_genesis

1. The Muaat player explores Avernus when they gain it.
2. The Muaat player may move Avernus even if another player controls it.
3. Avernus may be moved into a system containing another player’s units.
4. Structures may be placed on Avernus.
5. Any PDS (or other relevant unit) on Avernus may use itsSpace Cannonability during the movement step of the same tactical action the planet is moved.
6. Any of the Muaat player’s space docks (or other relevant units) on Avernus may use itsProductionability during the production step of the same tactical action the planet is moved.
