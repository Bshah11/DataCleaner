# Agenda: Anti-Intellectual Revolution

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_ANTI_INTELLECTUAL_REVOLUTION
* **Type**: Law
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: After a player researches a technology, he must destroy 1 of his non-fighter ships.
* **Effect Fail**: At the start of the next strategy phase, each player chooses and exhausts 1 planet for each technology he owns.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Players no longer need to destroy a non-fighter ship when researching technologies.
* **Clarifications**: ['Does affect: Researching technologies in any phase.', 'Does NOT affect: Gaining technologies.']
