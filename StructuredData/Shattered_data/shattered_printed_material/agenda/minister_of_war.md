# Agenda: Minister of War

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_MINISTER_OF_WAR
* **Type**: Law
* **Target**: Player
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: The elected player gains this card. The owner of this card may discard this card after performing an action to remove 1 of his command counters from the game board and return it to his reinforcements; then he may perform 1 additional action.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Player can no longer use the law.
* **Clarifications**: ["Effectively functions as an 'Unexpected Action' combined with 'Fleet Logistics'."]
