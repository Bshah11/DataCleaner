# Agenda: Arms Reduction

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_ARMS_REDUCTION
* **Type**: Directive
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: Each player destroys all but 2 of his dreadnoughts and all but 4 of his cruisers.
* **Effect Fail**: At the start of the next strategy phase, each player exhausts each of his planets that have a technology specialty.

* **Clarifications**: ['Regular Fleet Capacity rules apply.']
