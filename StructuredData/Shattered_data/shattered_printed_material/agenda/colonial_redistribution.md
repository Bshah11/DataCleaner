# Agenda: Colonial Redistribution

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_COLONIAL_REDISTRIBUTION
* **Type**: Directive
* **Target**: Non-Home Planet Other Than Mecatol Rex
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: Destroy each unit on the elected planet. Then, the player who controls that planet chooses 1 player with the fewest victory points; that player may place 1 infantry from his reinforcements on the elected planet.
## Metadata
* **Clarifications**: ['Taking the planet is optional for the player with fewest victory points.', "Can choose the target of 'Demilitarized Zone' agenda; cannot place an infantry on that planet."]
