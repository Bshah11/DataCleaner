# Agenda: Armed Forces Standardization

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_ARMED_FORCES_STANDARDIZATION
* **Type**: Directive
* **Target**: Player
* **Source**: pok
* **Effect Pass**: The elected player place command tokens from their reinforcements so that they have 3 tokens in their tactic pool, 3 tokens in their fleet pool, and 2 tokens in their strategy pool. They return any excess tokens to their reinforcements.
