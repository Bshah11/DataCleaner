# Agenda: Minister of Commerce

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_MINISTER_OF_COMMERCE
* **Type**: Law
* **Target**: Player
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: The elected player gains this card. After the owner of this card replenishes commodities, he gains 1 trade good for each player that is his neighbor.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Player no longer receives TGs when replenishing commodities.
