# Agenda: Judicial Abolishment

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_JUDICIAL_ABOLISHMENT
* **Type**: Directive
* **Target**: Law
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: Discard the elected law from play.
## Metadata
* **Reveal Condition**: When this agenda is revealed, if there are no laws in play, discard this card and reveal another agenda from the top of the deck.
* **Clarifications**: ["Has the same interaction as 'Repeal Law'."]
