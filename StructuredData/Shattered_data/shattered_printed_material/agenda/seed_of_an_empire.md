# Agenda: Seed of an Empire

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_SEED_OF_AN_EMPIRE
* **Type**: Directive
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: The player with the most victory points gains 1 victory point.
* **Effect Fail**: The player with the fewest victory points gains 1 victory point.
## Metadata
* **Clarifications**: ['In case of a tie, all eligible players gain the VP.']
