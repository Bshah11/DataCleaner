# Agenda: Wormhole Research

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_WORMHOLE_RESEARCH
* **Type**: Directive
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: Each player who has 1 or more ships in a system that contains a wormhole may research 1 technology. Then, destroy all ships in systems that contain an alpha or beta wormhole.
* **Effect Fail**: Each player that voted 'Against' removes 1 command token from his command sheet and returns it to his reinforcements.
## Metadata
* **Clarifications**: ['One technology regardless if one player has ships in more than 1 system that contains a wormhole.', 'Ghost of Creuss Delta Wormhole applies for the technology research window.', 'Ghost of Creuss Delta Wormhole does not require players to destroy ships.']
