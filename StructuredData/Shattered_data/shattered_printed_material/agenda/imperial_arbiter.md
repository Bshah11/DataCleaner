# Agenda: Imperial Arbiter

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_IMPERIAL_ARBITER
* **Type**: Law
* **Target**: Player
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: The elected player gains this card. At the end of the strategy phase, the owner of this card may discard this card to swap 1 of his strategy cards with 1 of another player's strategy cards.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Player can no longer use the card.
* **Clarifications**: ['Any TGs on the SC remain with the player that picked the SC first.', 'Response happens in clock-wise order from owner - relevant for Hacan with Quantum Datahub Node.']
