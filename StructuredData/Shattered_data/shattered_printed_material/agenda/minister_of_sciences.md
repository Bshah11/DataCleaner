# Agenda: Minister of Sciences

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_MINISTER_OF_SCIENCES
* **Type**: Law
* **Target**: Player
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: The elected player gains this card. When the owner of this card resolves the primary or secondary ability of the 'Technology' strategy card, he does not need to spend resources to research technology.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Player needs to spend resources when resolving the 'Technology' strategy card.
* **Clarifications**: ['Other research rules (prerequisites, etc.) still apply.']
