# Agenda: Political Censure

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_POLITICAL_CENSURE
* **Type**: Law
* **Target**: Player
* **Source**: pok
* **Effect Pass**: The elected player gains this card and 1 victory point. The elected player cannot play action cards.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Player loses 1 victory point and regains the ability to play action cards.
* **Clarifications**: ['If the owner loses this card, they lose 1 victory point immediately.']
