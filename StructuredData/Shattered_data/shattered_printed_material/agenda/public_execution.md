# Agenda: Public Execution

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_PUBLIC_EXECUTION
* **Type**: Directive
* **Target**: Player
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: The elected player discards all of his action cards. If he has the speaker token, he gives it to the player on his left. The elected player cannot vote on any agendas during this agenda phase.
