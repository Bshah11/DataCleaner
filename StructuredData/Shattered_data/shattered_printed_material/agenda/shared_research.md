# Agenda: Shared Research

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_SHARED_RESEARCH
* **Type**: Law
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: Each player's units can move through nebulae.
* **Effect Fail**: Each player places a command token from his reinforcements in his home system, if able.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Units follow regular nebulae movement rules.
* **Clarifications**: ['If a player has no tokens in reinforcements, they do not place one from elsewhere.']
