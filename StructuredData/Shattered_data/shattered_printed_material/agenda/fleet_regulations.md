# Agenda: Fleet Regulations

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_FLEET_REGULATIONS
* **Type**: Law
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: Each player cannot have more than 4 tokens in his fleet pool.
* **Effect Fail**: Each player places 1 command token from his reinforcements in his fleet pool.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Each player can have as many tokens in his fleet pool as they have on their command sheet.
