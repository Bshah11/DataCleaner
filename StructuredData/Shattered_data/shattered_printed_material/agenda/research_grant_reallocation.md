# Agenda: Research Grant Reallocation

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_RESEARCH_GRANT_REALLOCATION
* **Type**: Directive
* **Target**: Player
* **Source**: pok
* **Effect Pass**: The elected player gains any 1 technology. For each prerequisite on that technology, they remove 1 token from their fleet pool and return it to reinforcements.
## Metadata
* **Clarifications**: ['Prerequisites are not required to gain the tech, but they determine the cost in fleet tokens.']
