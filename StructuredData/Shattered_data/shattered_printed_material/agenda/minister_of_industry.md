# Agenda: Minister of Industry

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_MINISTER_OF_INDUSTRY
* **Type**: Law
* **Target**: Player
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: The elected player gains this card. When the owner of this card places a space dock in a system, his units in that system may use their PRODUCTION abilities.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Player's units can no longer use PRODUCTION abilities when placing a space dock.
* **Clarifications**: ['Can use the PRODUCTION ability of ALL units in the system.', "Cannot be used together with effects that 'replace' units."]
