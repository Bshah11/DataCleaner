# Agenda: Nexus Sovereignty

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_NEXUS_SOVEREIGNTY
* **Type**: Law
* **Source**: pok
* **Effect Pass**: Alpha and beta wormholes in the wormhole nexus have no effect during movement.
* **Effect Fail**: Place a gamma wormhole token in the Mecatol Rex system.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Wormholes in the Nexus regain movement effects.
* **Clarifications**: ['If the fail effect resolves, the Gamma wormhole remains even if this law is not in play (since it was an immediate effect).']
