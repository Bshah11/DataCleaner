# Agenda: Rearmament Agreement

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_REARMAMENT_AGREEMENT
* **Type**: Directive
* **Source**: pok
* **Effect Pass**: Each player places 1 mech from their reinforcements on a planet they control in their home system.
* **Effect Fail**: Each player replaces each of their mechs with 1 infantry from their reinforcements.
## Metadata
* **Clarifications**: ['If a player has no mechs in reinforcements for the pass effect, they place nothing.']
