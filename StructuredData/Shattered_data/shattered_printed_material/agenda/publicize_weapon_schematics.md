# Agenda: Publicize Weapon Schematics

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_PUBLICIZE_WEAPON_SCHEMATICS
* **Type**: Law
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: If any player owns a war sun technology, all players may ignore all prerequisites on war sun technologies. All war suns lose Sustain Damage.
* **Effect Fail**: Each player that owns a war sun technology discards all of his action cards.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Players no longer ignore War Sun prerequisites. War Suns regain Sustain Damage.
* **Clarifications**: ['Technology still needs to be researched to build War Suns.', "Muaat's Prototype War Sun I does NOT count as a WS technology for the 'fail' condition; PWS II does."]
