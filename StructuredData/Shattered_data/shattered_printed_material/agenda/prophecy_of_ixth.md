# Agenda: Prophecy of Ixth

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_PROPHECY_OF_IXTH
* **Type**: Law
* **Target**: Player
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: The elected player gains this card. The owner of this card applies +1 to the result of his fighter's combat rolls. When the owner of this card uses PRODUCTION, he discards this card unless he produces 2 or more fighters.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Fighters no longer get +1 to combat rolls.
* **Clarifications**: ["2 fighters need to be produced for EVERY unit that uses 'PRODUCTION' in the system, including Letani Warriors."]
