# Agenda: Representative Government

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_REPRESENTATIVE_GOVERNMENT
* **Type**: Law
* **Source**: pok
* **Effect Pass**: Players cannot exhaust planets to cast votes; each player may cast 1 vote on each agenda instead. Players cannot cast additional votes.
* **Effect Fail**: At the start of the next strategy phase, each player that voted 'Against' exhausts all of their cultural planets.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Standard voting rules (spending influence) resume.
* **Clarifications**: ["This overrides abilities that grant extra votes (like 'Distinguished Councilor')."]
