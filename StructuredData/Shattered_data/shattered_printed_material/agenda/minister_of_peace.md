# Agenda: Minister of Peace

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_MINISTER_OF_PEACE
* **Type**: Law
* **Target**: Player
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: The elected player gains this card. After a player activates a system that contains 1 or more of a different player's units, the owner of this card may discard this card; immediately end the active player's turn.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Player can no longer use the law.
