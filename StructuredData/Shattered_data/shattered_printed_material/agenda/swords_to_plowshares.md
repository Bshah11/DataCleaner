# Agenda: Swords to Plowshares

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_SWORDS_TO_PLOWSHARES
* **Type**: Directive
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: Each player destroys half of his infantry on each planet he controls, rounded up. Then, each player gains trade goods equal to the number of his infantry that were destroyed.
* **Effect Fail**: Each player places 1 infantry from his reinforcements on each planet he controls.
## Metadata
* **Clarifications**: ['Infantry II (Gen Synthesis) units roll for survival.']
