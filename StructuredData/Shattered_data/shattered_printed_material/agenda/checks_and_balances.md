# Agenda: Checks and Balances

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_CHECKS_AND_BALANCES
* **Type**: Law
* **Source**: pok
* **Effect Pass**: When a player chooses a strategy card during the strategy phase, they give that strategy card to another player who does not have 1 (or a player who does not have 2 in a 3 or 4-player game), if able.
* **Effect Fail**: Each player readies only 3 of their planets at the end of this agenda phase.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Standard strategy card drafting resumes.
