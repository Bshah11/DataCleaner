# Agenda: Search Warrant

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_SEARCH_WARRANT
* **Type**: Law
* **Target**: Player
* **Source**: pok
* **Effect Pass**: The elected player gains this card and draws 2 secret objectives. The owner plays with their secret objectives revealed.
