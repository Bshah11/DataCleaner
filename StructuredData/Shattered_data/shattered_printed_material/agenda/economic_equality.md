# Agenda: Economic Equality

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_ECONOMIC_EQUALITY
* **Type**: Directive
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: Each player returns all of his trade goods to the supply. Then, each player gains 5 trade goods.
* **Effect Fail**: Each player returns all of his trade goods to the supply.
## Metadata
* **Clarifications**: ['Happens before riders.', 'Trade rider comes into effect after the agenda resolves.']
