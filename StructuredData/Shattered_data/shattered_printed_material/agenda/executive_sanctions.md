# Agenda: Executive Sanctions

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_EXECUTIVE_SANCTIONS
* **Type**: Law
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: Each player can have a maximum of 3 action cards in his hand.
* **Effect Fail**: Each player discards 1 random action card from his hand.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Each player follows the regular hand-size limit.
* **Clarifications**: ['The Yssaril Tribes are not affected by the limitation.']
