# Agenda: Articles of War

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_ARTICLES_OF_WAR
* **Type**: Law
* **Source**: pok
* **Effect Pass**: All mechs lose their printed abilities except for Sustain Damage.
* **Effect Fail**: Each player that voted 'For' gets 3 trade goods.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Mechs regain their printed abilities.
* **Clarifications**: ['Mechs still count as mechs for unit requirements.']
