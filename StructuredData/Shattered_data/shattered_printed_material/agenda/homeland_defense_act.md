# Agenda: Homeland Defense Act

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_HOMELAND_DEFENSE_ACT
* **Type**: Law
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: Each player can have any number of PDS units on planets he controls.
* **Effect Fail**: Each player destroys 1 of his PDS units.
## Metadata
* **Repeal Interaction**: Destroy down to 2 PDS on each planet.
