# Agenda: Classified Document Leaks

* **Card Id**: AGENDA_CLASSIFIED_DOCUMENT_LEAKS
* **Type**: Law
* **Target**: Scored Secret Objective
* **Source**: base
* **Effect Pass**: The elected secret objective becomes a public objective; place it near the other public objectives in the common play area.
## Metadata
* **Reveal Condition**: When this agenda is revealed, if there are no scored secret objectives, discard this card and reveal another agenda from the top of the deck.
* **Repeal Interaction**: Complex interactions with scored/unscored SO limits for the original owner.
* **Clarifications**: ['The chosen SO no longer counts towards the regular limit of 3 SOs for the player who originally scored it.']
