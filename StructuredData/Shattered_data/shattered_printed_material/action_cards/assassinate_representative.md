# Action Cards: Assassinate Representative

* **Card Id**: AC_ASSASSINATE_REPRESENTATIVE
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: AGENDA
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: After an agenda is revealed
* **Effect**: Choose 1 player. That player cannot vote on this agenda.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: After the player is chosen.
* **Clarifications**: ['Can be played on a player with 0 influence.', 'Target player can still play Riders.', 'Cannot target self.']
