# Action Cards: Confusing Legal Text

* **Card Id**: AC_CONFUSING_LEGAL_TEXT
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: AGENDA
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: When you are elected as the outcome of an agenda
* **Effect**: Choose 1 player. That player is the elected player instead.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: After the player is chosen.
* **Clarifications**: ['Riders use the target of Confusing Legal Text as the final outcome.', 'Cannot target self.']
