# Action Cards: Plague

* **Card Id**: AC_PLAGUE
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: ACTION
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: ACTION: Choose 1 planet that is controlled by another player.
* **Effect**: Roll 1 die for each infantry on that planet. For each result of 6 or greater, destroy 1 of those units.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: After choosing the planet.
* **Clarifications**: ['Can play on a planet with 0 GFs on it.', 'GF II rolls for survival.', 'Cannot target own planets.']
