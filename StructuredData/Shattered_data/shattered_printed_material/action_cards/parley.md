# Action Cards: Parley

* **Card Id**: AC_PARLEY
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: ACTION (INVASION)
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: After another player commits units to land on a planet you control
* **Effect**: Return the committed units to the space area.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: After card is played.
* **Clarifications**: ['Units returned to Space area cannot be redeployed.']
