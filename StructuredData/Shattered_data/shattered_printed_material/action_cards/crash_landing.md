# Action Cards: Crash Landing

* **Card Id**: AC_CRASH_LANDING
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: POST_SPACE_COMBAT
* **Source**: Thunder's Edge
* **Timing**: When your last ship in the active system is destroyed
* **Effect**: Place 1 of your ground forces from the space area of the active system onto a planet in that system other than Mecatol Rex; if the planet contains other players' units, place your ground force into coexistence.
