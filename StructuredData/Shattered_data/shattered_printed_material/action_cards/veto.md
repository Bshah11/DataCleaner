# Action Cards: Veto

* **Card Id**: AC_VETO
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: AGENDA
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: When an agenda is revealed
* **Effect**: Discard that agenda and reveal 1 agenda from the top of the deck. Players vote on this agenda instead.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: After card is played.
* **Clarifications**: ['ERRATA: Happens before riders.']
