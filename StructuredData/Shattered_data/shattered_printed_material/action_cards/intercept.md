# Action Cards: Intercept

* **Card Id**: AC_INTERCEPT
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: ACTION (SPACE COMBAT)
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: After your opponent declares a retreat during a space combat
* **Effect**: Your opponent cannot retreat during this round of space combat.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: After card is played.
* **Clarifications**: ['Cannot be used after a Skilled Retreat.']
