# Action Cards: Courageous to the End

* **Card Id**: AC_COURAGEOUS_TO_THE_END
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: ACTION (SPACE COMBAT)
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: After 1 of your ships is destroyed during a space combat
* **Effect**: Roll 2 dice. For each result equal to or greater than that ship's combat value, your opponent must choose and destroy 1 of his ships.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: Before making the rolls.
* **Clarifications**: ['Card is still part of the combat round.', 'Nekro steals a tech even if it was played on last ship.']
