# Action Cards: Politics Rider

* **Card Id**: AC_POLITICS_RIDER
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: AGENDA
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: After an agenda is revealed
* **Effect**: You cannot vote on this agenda. Predict aloud an outcome of this agenda. If your prediction is correct, draw 3 action cards and gain the speaker token.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: After the outcome is chosen.
* **Clarifications**: ['Put on discard pile before voting.']
