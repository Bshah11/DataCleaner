# Action Cards: Experimental Battlestation

* **Card Id**: AC_EXPERIMENTAL_BATTLESTATION
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: ACTION (MOVEMENT)
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: After another player moves ships into a system during a tactical ACTION: Choose 1 of your space docks that is either in or adjacent to that system.
* **Effect**: That space dock uses SPACE CANNON 5 (x3) against ships in the active system.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: Before making rolls.
* **Clarifications**: ['Part of SPACE CANNON Offense.', 'Can benefit from Plasma Scoring.']
