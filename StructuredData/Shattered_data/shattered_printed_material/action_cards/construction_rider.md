# Action Cards: Construction Rider

* **Card Id**: AC_CONSTRUCTION_RIDER
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: AGENDA
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: After an agenda is revealed
* **Effect**: You cannot vote on this agenda. Predict aloud an outcome of this agenda. If your prediction is correct, place 1 space dock from your reinforcements on a planet you control.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: After the outcome is chosen.
