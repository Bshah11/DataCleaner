* **Card Id**: AC_RESCUE
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: POST_MOVEMENT
* **Source**: Thunder's Edge
* **Timing**: After another player moves ships into a system that contains your ships
* **Effect**: You may move 1 of your ships into the active system from any system that does not contain one of your command tokens.
