# Action Cards: Public Disgrace

* **Card Id**: AC_PUBLIC_DISGRACE
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: STRATEGY
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: When another player chooses a strategy card during the strategy phase
* **Effect**: That player must choose a different strategy card instead, if able.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: After card is played.
* **Clarifications**: ['TGs on disgraced SC remain on that SC.']
