# Action Cards: Infiltrate

* **Card Id**: AC_INFILTRATE
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: ACTION (INVASION)
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: When you gain control of a planet
* **Effect**: Replace each PDS and space dock that is on that planet with a matching unit from your reinforcements.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: Before placing units on the board.
* **Clarifications**: ['Does NOT trigger Minister of Industry law.', 'Floating Factories cannot be infiltrated while in the space area.']
