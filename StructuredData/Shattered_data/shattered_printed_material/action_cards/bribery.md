# Action Cards: Bribery

* **Card Id**: AC_BRIBERY
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: AGENDA
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: After the speaker votes on an agenda
* **Effect**: Spend any number of trade goods. For each trade good spent, cast 1 additional vote for any outcome.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: After the amount of TGs is decided.
* **Clarifications**: ['TGs are not lost this way.', 'Can play if Speaker abstains or cannot vote.', 'Must vote for same outcome.']
