# Action Cards: Fire Team

* **Card Id**: AC_FIRE_TEAM
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: ACTION (INVASION)
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: After your ground forces make combat rolls during a round of ground combat
* **Effect**: Reroll any number of your dice.
