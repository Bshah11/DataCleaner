# Action Cards: Emergency Repairs

* **Card Id**: AC_EMERGENCY_REPAIRS
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: ACTION (SPACE COMBAT)
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: At the start or end of a combat round
* **Effect**: Repair all of your units that have Sustain Damage in the active system.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: After choosing sustained ships.
* **Clarifications**: ['Can play if: Player has NO units that have Sustain Damage.']
