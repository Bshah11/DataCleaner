# Action Cards: In The Silence Of Space

* **Card Id**: AC_IN_THE_SILENCE_OF_SPACE
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Phase**: ACTION (ACTIVATION)
* **Source**: Base
* **Timing**: After you activate a system
* **Effect**: Choose 1 system. During this tactical action, your ships in the chosen system can move through systems that contain other players' ships.
## Metadata
* **Sabotage Window**: After choosing the system.
* **Clarifications**: ['Valid for ships coming out of the chosen system only.']
