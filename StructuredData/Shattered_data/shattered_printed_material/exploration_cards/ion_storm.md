# Exploration Cards: Ion Storm

* **Card Id**: EXP_FRON_ION_STORM
* **Deck Type**: Frontier
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Text**: Place the ion storm token in this system with either side faceup. Then, place this card in the common play area. At the end of the 'Move Ships' or 'Retreat' substep of a tactical action during which 1 or more of your ships use the ion storm wormhole, flip the ion storm token to its opposing side.
## Metadata
* **Ruling Notes**: The card remains in the common area as a rule reminder for all players. The wormhole's adjacency changes dynamically as ships pass through.
