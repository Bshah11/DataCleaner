# Exploration Cards: Demilitarized Zone

* **Card Id**: EXP_CUL_DEMILITARIZED_ZONE
* **Deck Type**: Cultural
* **Quantity**: 1
* **Text**: Return all structures on this planet to your reinforcements. Then, return all ground forces on this planet to the space area. Attach: Units cannot be committed to, produced on, or placed on this planet. During the agenda phase, this planet's planet card can be traded as part of a transaction.
## Metadata
* **Ruling Notes**: This attachment prevents all forms of unit placement (production, abilities, or commitment). The trading rule is an exception to standard transaction restrictions.
