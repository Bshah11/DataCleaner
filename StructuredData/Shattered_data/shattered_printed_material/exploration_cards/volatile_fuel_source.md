# Exploration Cards: Volatile Fuel Source

* **Card Id**: EXP_HAZ_VOLATILE_FUEL_SOURCE
* **Deck Type**: Hazardous
* **Quantity**: 3
* **Text**: If you have at least 1 mech on this planet, or if you remove 1 infantry from this planet, gain 1 command token.
## Metadata
* **Flavor**: It turned out that the continuous lightning storms were an unlimited source of power...
* **Ruling Notes**: Removing an infantry is a cost. Having a mech is an alternative condition that waives the cost.
