# Exploration Cards: Local Fabricators

* **Card Id**: EXP_IND_LOCAL_FABRICATORS
* **Deck Type**: Industrial
* **Quantity**: 4
* **Text**: You may gain 1 commodity, or you may spend 1 trade good or 1 commodity to place 1 mech from your reinforcements on this planet.
## Metadata
* **Ruling Notes**: Placing a mech is not 'producing' a mech. This ignores production limits but requires the unit to be available in reinforcements.
