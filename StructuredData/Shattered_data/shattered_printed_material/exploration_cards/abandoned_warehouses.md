# Exploration Cards: Abandoned Warehouses

* **Card Id**: EXP_IND_ABANDONED_WAREHOUSES
* **Deck Type**: Industrial
* **Quantity**: 4
* **Text**: You may gain 2 commodities, or you may convert up to 2 of your commodities to trade goods.
## Metadata
* **Ruling Notes**: Gaining commodities cannot exceed the player's commodity limit. Conversion is a direct flip to trade goods.
