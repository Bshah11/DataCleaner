# Strategy Cards: Politics

* **Card Id**: STRAT_POLITICS
* **Initiative**: 3
## Primary
* **Speaker Token**: Choose a player other than the speaker. That player gains the speaker token.
* **Draw Action Cards**: Draw 2 action cards.
* **Agenda Manipulation**: Look at the top 2 cards of the agenda deck. Place each card on the top or bottom of the deck in any order.
## Secondary
* **Draw Action Cards**: Spend 1 token from your strategy pool to draw 2 action cards.
