# Strategy Cards: Imperial

* **Card Id**: STRAT_IMPERIAL
* **Initiative**: 8
## Primary
* **Score Public**: Immediately score 1 public objective if you fulfill its requirements.
* **Mecatol Bonus**: Gain 1 victory point if you control Mecatol Rex; otherwise, draw 1 secret objective.
## Secondary
* **Draw Secret**: Spend 1 token from your strategy pool to draw 1 secret objective.
