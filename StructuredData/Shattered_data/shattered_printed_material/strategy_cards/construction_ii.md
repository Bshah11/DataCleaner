# Strategy Cards: Construction II

* **Card Id**: STRAT_CONSTRUCTION
* **Initiative**: 4
## Primary
* **Place Structure**: Either place 1 structure on a planet you control, or use the PRODUCTION ability of 1 of your space docks.
* **Bonus Structure**: Place 1 structure on a planet you control.
## Secondary
* **Place Structure**: Spend 1 token from your strategy pool to place 1 structure on a planet you control.
