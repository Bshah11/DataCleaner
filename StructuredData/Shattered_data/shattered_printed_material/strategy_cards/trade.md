# Strategy Cards: Trade

* **Card Id**: STRAT_TRADE
* **Initiative**: 5
## Primary
* **Gain Goods**: Gain 3 trade goods.
* **Replenish**: Replenish commodities.
* **Negotiation**: Choose any number of other players. Those players use the secondary ability of this strategy card without spending a command token.
## Secondary
* **Replenish**: Spend 1 token from your strategy pool to replenish commodities.
