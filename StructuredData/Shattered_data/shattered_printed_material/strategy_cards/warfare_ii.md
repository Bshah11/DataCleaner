# Strategy Cards: Warfare II

* **Card Id**: STRAT_WARFARE
* **Initiative**: 6
## Primary
* **Free Tactical Action**: Perform a tactical action in any system without placing a command token, even if already activated.
* **Redistribution**: You may redistribute your command tokens before and after this action.
## Secondary
* **Home Production**: Spend 1 token from your strategy pool to use the PRODUCTION abilities of the units in your home system.
