# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Agendas
## TYPE: Agenda
### COMPONENT: Ixthian Artifact
#### ID: agendas_ixthian_artifact | Source: Base Game

1. A player must still meet the prerequisites for the technology they research.
2. A player with three or fewer units in a system adjacent to the Mecatol Rex system destroys all of their units in that system. Otherwise, a player chooses which three units are destroyed.
3. All units are destroyed simultaneously.
4. To cast votes against or to play a rider onIxthian Artifactis to commit heresy.
