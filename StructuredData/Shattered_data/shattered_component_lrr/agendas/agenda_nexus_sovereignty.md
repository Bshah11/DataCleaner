# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Agendas
## TYPE: Agenda
### COMPONENT: Nexus Sovereignty
#### ID: agendas_nexus_sovereignty | Source: Base Game

1. The effect of theNexus Sovereigntylaw does not affect the Creuss player.
2. When theNexus Sovereigntylaw is in play, units cannot move through the alpha and beta wormholes in the wormhole nexus, even by effects that move ships outside of the movement step of a tactical action, such as while retreating.
3. When theNexus Sovereigntylaw is in play, hits may still be produced against ships in the active system by units with theDeep Space Cannonability in systems that are adjacent via a wormhole during theSpace Cannon Offensestep of a tactical action.
4. Players may still be neighbors via systems that are adjacent via wormholes.
