# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Agendas
## TYPE: Agenda
### COMPONENT: Committee Formation
#### ID: agendas_committee_formation | Source: Base Game

1. Committee Formationis resolved after any riders have been played, if the owner so chooses. Riders will resolve if the predicted outcome matches the player elected by the effect ofCommittee Formation.
2. TheConfusing Legal TextandConfounding Legal Textaction cards may be played afterCommittee Formationhas been discarded to change who the elected player is, which will negate the effects ofCommittee Formation.
