# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Agendas
## TYPE: Agenda
### COMPONENT: Colonial Redistribution
#### ID: agendas_colonial_redistribution | Source: Base Game

1. If the player that originally controlled the elected planet has the fewest victory points, they may choose themselves to place an infantry on that planet.
2. If the elected planet has theDemilitarized Zoneexploration card attached, no infantry will be placed on it. Control of the planet will remain with the original player.
