# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Agendas
## TYPE: Agenda
### COMPONENT: Minister of Industry
#### ID: agendas_minister_of_industry | Source: Base Game

1. All units in that system may use theirProductionability, not just the placed space dock.
2. The produced units must be paid for.
3. A space dock cannot be placed on a planet already containing a space dock.
4. An ability that puts a space dock on the game board by “replacing”, rather than “placing”, such as theInfiltrateaction card, will triggerMinister of Industry.
