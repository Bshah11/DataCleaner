# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Relics
## TYPE: Relic
### COMPONENT: Circlet of the Void
#### ID: relics_circlet_of_the_void | Source: Base Game

1. The player that ownsCirclet of the Voidmay move through or into supernovae and/or asteroid fields.
2. The player that ownsCirclet of the Voidmay move through nebulae, and their ships in nebulae do not have their movement values reduced.
3. The player that ownsCirclet of the Voidstill applies the movement bonus to their ships that will move through gravity rifts.
4. The player that ownsCirclet of the Voidwill still ignore anomaly movement effects and not roll for gravity rifts ifCirclet of the Voidis exhausted.
