### COMPONENT: Shard of the Throne
#### ID: relics_shard_of_the_throne | Source: Base Game

1. When the player that owns theShard of the Throneloses control of a legendary planet or a planet in their home system, they immediately give it to the player who gained control of that planet. No abilities may be resolved before this.
2. If the player that gainsShard of the Thronecontrols no legendary planets or planets in their home system, they cannot lose it.
