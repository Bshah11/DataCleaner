# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Maneuvering Jets
#### ID: action_cards_maneuvering_jets | Source: Base Game

1. Maneuvering Jetscannot be used to cancel hits during combat.
2. Maneuvering Jetsmay cancel hits produced during either theSpace Cannon Offenseor theSpace Cannon Defensestep.
3. A player cannot play a secondManeuvering Jetsto cancel a second hit produced by the same Space Cannon roll.
4. Maneuvering Jetsmay cancel hits produced due to theExperimental Battlestationaction card.
