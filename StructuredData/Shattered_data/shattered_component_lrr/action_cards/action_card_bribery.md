# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Bribery
#### ID: action_cards_bribery | Source: Base Game

1. Briberymay be played after the speaker has abstained, or would vote if they are unable to.
2. A player may spend zero trade goods when they playBribery.
3. A player must have cast votes for an outcome to playBribery, and the additional votes must be cast for the same outcome.
4. The speaker may playDistinguished Councilorafter another player playsBribery.
5. Briberyis played before any effects that change the outcome of an agenda.
