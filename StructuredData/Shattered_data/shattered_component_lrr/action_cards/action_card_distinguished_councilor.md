# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Distinguished Councilor
#### ID: action_cards_distinguished_councilor | Source: Base Game

1. A player must have cast votes for an outcome to playDistinguished Councilor, and the additional votes must be cast for the same outcome.
2. A player may only playDistinguished Councilorafter their normal vote, not after they cast additional votes at another point.
3. The speaker may playDistinguished Councilorafter another player playsBribery.
4. Distinguished Counciloris played before any effects that change the outcome of an agenda.
