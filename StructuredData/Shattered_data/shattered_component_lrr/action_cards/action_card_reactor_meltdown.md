# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Reactor Meltdown
#### ID: action_cards_reactor_meltdown | Source: Base Game

1. A player cannot destroy their own space dock withReactor Meltdown.
2. At least one space dock belonging to another player must exist on the game board in a non–home system for a player to play and resolveReactor Meltdown.
3. If a player owns a space dock in another player’s home system, that space dock cannot be targeted byReactor Meltdown.
