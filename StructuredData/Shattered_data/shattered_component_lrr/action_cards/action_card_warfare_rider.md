# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Warfare Rider
#### ID: action_cards_warfare_rider | Source: Base Game

1. After playing aWarfare Rider, a player may use additional abilities, including playing other rider cards.
2. The effect of a successfulWarfare Riderwill be resolved after the effect of the agenda.
3. A player who cannot vote may still play aWarfare Rider.
4. If a player wishes to place a dreadnought, but there are none in their reinforcements, they may remove a dreadnought from any system that does not contain one of their command tokens and place that instead. This dreadnought will be placed undamaged.
