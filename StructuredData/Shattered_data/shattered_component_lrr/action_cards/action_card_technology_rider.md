# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Technology Rider
#### ID: action_cards_technology_rider | Source: Base Game

1. After playing aTechnology Rider, a player may use additional abilities, including playing other rider cards.
2. The effect of a successfulTechnology Riderwill be resolved after the effect of the agenda.
3. A player who cannot vote may still play aTechnology Rider.
4. A player must still meet the prerequisites for the technology they research.
