# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Trade Rider
#### ID: action_cards_trade_rider | Source: Base Game

1. After playing aTrade Rider, a player may use additional abilities, including playing other rider cards.
2. The effect of a successfulTrade Riderwill be resolved after the effect of the agenda.
3. A player who cannot vote may still play aTrade Rider.
