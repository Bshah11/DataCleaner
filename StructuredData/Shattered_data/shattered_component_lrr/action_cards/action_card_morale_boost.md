# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Morale Boost
#### ID: action_cards_morale_boost | Source: Base Game

1. The +1 will apply to any rerolls during the combat round.
2. A secondMorale Boostcannot be played during a combat round to give +2 to the combat rolls of a player’s units.
3. Morale Boosthas no effect on anti–fighter barrage rolls.
