# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Public Disgrace
#### ID: action_cards_public_disgrace | Source: Base Game

1. If the originally chosen strategy card has trade goods on it, those will not be taken ifPublic Disgraceis played.
2. Public Disgracemay only be played during the regular choosing of a strategy card during the strategy phase. It cannot be played when other effects cause a player to take a strategy card, such as theImperial Arbiteragenda.
