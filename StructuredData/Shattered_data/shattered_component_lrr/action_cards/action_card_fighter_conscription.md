# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Fighter Conscription
#### ID: action_cards_fighter_conscription | Source: Base Game

1. Only one fighter will be placed in a system containing both a space dock and a ship with capacity.
2. A player may place a fighter in a system that is at its capacity limit. If they do so, they must then remove a fighter or ground force from the space area of that system.
