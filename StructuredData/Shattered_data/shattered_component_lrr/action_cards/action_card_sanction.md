# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Sanction
#### ID: action_cards_sanction | Source: Base Game

1. After playing aSanction, a player may use additional abilities, including playing rider cards.
2. The effect of a successfulSanctionwill be resolved after the effect of the agenda.
3. A player who cannot vote may still play aSanction.
