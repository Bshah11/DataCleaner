# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Unstable Planet
#### ID: action_cards_unstable_planet | Source: Base Game

1. A player may select an exhausted planet, and they may select a planet with fewer than three infantry on it (including zero).
2. If the selected planet has three or fewer infantry on it, all infantry will be destroyed. Otherwise, three infantry will be destroyed.
