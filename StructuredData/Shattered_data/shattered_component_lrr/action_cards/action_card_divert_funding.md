# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Divert Funding
#### ID: action_cards_divert_funding | Source: Base Game

1. A player must have at least one non–unit upgrade, non–faction technology to play and resolveDivert Funding.
2. A player must still meet the prerequisites for the technology they research.
3. The researched technology may be a unit upgrade technology, and it may be a faction technology.
4. The researched technology cannot be the returned technology.
5. Other players must decide whether to playSabotage, or otherwise cancel the action card, before the player playingDivert Fundingannounces which technology they are researching.
6. If a player later gains the technology they returned, they gain it readied.
7. If the player returns theAntimass Deflectorstechnology, their units may remain in and move out of any asteroid fields they are currently in, but cannot move additional units into or through.
