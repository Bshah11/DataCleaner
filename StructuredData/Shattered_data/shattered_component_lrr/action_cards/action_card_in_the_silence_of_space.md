# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: In The Silence Of Space
#### ID: action_cards_in_the_silence_of_space | Source: Base Game

1. Only the ships in the chosen system may move through systems that contain other players’ ships. Ships in other systems may still move to the active system if they are able to do so without moving through systems that contain other players’ ships.
