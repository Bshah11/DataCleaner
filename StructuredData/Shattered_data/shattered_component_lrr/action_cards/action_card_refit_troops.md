# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Refit Troops
#### ID: action_cards_refit_troops | Source: Base Game

1. A player must have at least one infantry on the game board to play and resolveRefit Troops.
2. If a player has no mechs left in their reinforcements, they may remove a mech from any system that does not contain one of their command tokens and place that instead. The mech will be placed undamaged.
