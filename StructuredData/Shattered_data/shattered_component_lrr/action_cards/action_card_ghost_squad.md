# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Action Cards
## TYPE: Action_card
### COMPONENT: Ghost Squad
#### ID: action_cards_ghost_squad | Source: Base Game

1. Ghost Squadis played after the active player has assigned which planet each of their ground forces are being committed to. They cannot modify this afterGhost Squadhas been played.
2. Ghost Squadmay be played whenever ground forces are committed, such as from the Yin player’sQuantum Dissemination Ωhero ability.
3. The Ul player cannot useGhost Squadto move a Hel–Titan.
4. A player may move zero ground forces withGhost Squad.
