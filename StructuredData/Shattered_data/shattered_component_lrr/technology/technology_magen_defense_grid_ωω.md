# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Technology
## TYPE: Technology
### COMPONENT: Magen Defense Grid ΩΩ
#### ID: technology_magen_defense_grid | Source: Codex

1. The player withMagen Defense Grid ΩΩchooses which ground force the hit is assigned to.
2. The chosen ground force may cancel the hit by using itsSustain Damageability, if present. A different ground force with theSustain Damageability cannot do so.
3. If the last of a player’s ground forces in a ground combat are destroyed by the effect ofMagen Defense Grid ΩΩ, that player loses the combat immediately. They will be unable to use any “start of combat” abilities.
4. Magen Defense Grid ΩΩwill place infantry regardless of who activates the system.
5. If a player has multiple structures in the system, one infantry is placed with each.
6. The infantry must be placed on the same planet as the structure.
