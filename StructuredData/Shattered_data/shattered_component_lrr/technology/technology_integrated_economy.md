# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Technology
## TYPE: Technology
### COMPONENT: Integrated Economy
#### ID: technology_integrated_economy | Source: Base Game

1. The produced units must be paid for.
2. There is no limit on the number of units produced, so long as the combined cost does not exceed the planet’s resource value.
3. Two fighters or two infantry have a combined cost of one resource.
4. A player may produce ships withIntegrated Economy, unless another player has ships in the space area of that planet’s system.
