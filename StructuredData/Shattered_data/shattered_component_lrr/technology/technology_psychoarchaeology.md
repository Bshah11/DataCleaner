# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Technology
## TYPE: Technology
### COMPONENT: Psychoarchaeology
#### ID: technology_psychoarchaeology | Source: Base Game

1. When researching a technology, each planet with a technology speciality can only be used to ignore a single prerequisite.
2. Exhausting planets to gain trade goods can be done at any time during action phase, except when another ability is being resolved. For example, if a player has one of their planets with a technology specialty targeted with theUprisingaction card, they cannot usePsychoarchaeologyin response to gain a trade good.
3. If multiple planets are exhausted, the trade goods are gained one at a time.
