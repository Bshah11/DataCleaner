# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Objectives
## TYPE: Objective
### COMPONENT: Learn the Secrets of the Cosmos
#### ID: objectives_learn_the_secrets_of_the_cosmos | Source: Base Game

1. The three systems may be mutually adjacent to one system, or separate systems.
2. Having ships in an anomaly is irrelevant, unless that system is also adjacent to another anomaly.
3. Any ground forces or structures are irrelevant.
4. Any system containing a Vuil’raith player’s Dimensional Tear is a gravity rift, and thus, an anomaly.
