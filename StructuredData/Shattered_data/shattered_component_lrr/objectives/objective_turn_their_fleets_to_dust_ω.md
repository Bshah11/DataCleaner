# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Objectives
## TYPE: Objective
### COMPONENT: Turn Their Fleets to Dust Ω
#### ID: objectives_turn_their_fleets_to_dust | Source: Codex

1. If the last ship is destroyed using aDirect Hitaction card,Turn Their Fleets to Dust Ωmay be scored.
2. If a player still has fighters in a system after assigning all hits produced during theSpace Cannon Offensestep,Turn Their Fleets to Dust Ωmay be scored.
3. If a player’s last ship in a system is destroyed, but an ability allows that player to then place ships into that system,Turn Their Fleets to Dust Ωmay still be scored.
4. ScoringTurn Their Fleets to Dust Ωwill not prevent that player from scoring a secret objective in theSpace Combatstep during this tactical action.
5. If a system contains only fighters, thenTurn Their Fleets to Dust Ωcannot be scored by destroying them.
6. Any effect that destroys non–fighter ships during theSpace Cannon Offensestep may be used to scoreTurn Their Fleets to Dust Ω, not just hits produced by a space cannon roll.
