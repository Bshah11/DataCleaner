# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Objectives
## TYPE: Objective
### COMPONENT: Protect the Border
#### ID: objectives_protect_the_border | Source: Base Game

1. The number of structures on the planet is irrelevant, if there is at least one.
2. The structures may be on planets in another player’s home system.
3. The Saar player’s Floating Factories cannot be used to scoreProtect the Border.
