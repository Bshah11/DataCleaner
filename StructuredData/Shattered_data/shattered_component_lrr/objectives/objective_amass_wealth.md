# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Objectives
## TYPE: Objective
### COMPONENT: Amass Wealth
#### ID: objectives_amass_wealth | Source: Base Game

1. The influence, resources and trade goods must be spent during the status phase. Any that were spent during the action phase will have no effect.
2. A planet may be exhausted to spend either its resource or influence value, but not both.
3. Trade goods may be spent as influence or resources when scoringAmass Wealth.
