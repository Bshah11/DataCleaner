## TYPE: Objective
### COMPONENT: Demonstrate Your Power
#### ID: objectives_demonstrate_your_power | Source: Base Game

1. A player need not win the combat to scoreDemonstrate Your Power; they are able to score it if their opponent plays theSkilled Retreataction card.
2. A player involved in a combat due to the Mahact player’sBenedictionhero ability may scoreDemonstrate Your Powerafter that combat.
3. Units that are temporarily ships for the duration of a space combat, such as those made so by the Nekro player’s flagship,The Alastor, or the Naaz–Rokha player’s Z–Grav Eidolon mechs, may count towards scoringDemonstrate Your Power.
4. The Mentak player may use theSleeper Cellability of their hero, during the last round of combat to place ships before scoringDemonstrate Your Power. However, fleet pool limits are also checked before scoring.
