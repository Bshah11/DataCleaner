# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Exploration Cards
## TYPE: Exploration_card
### COMPONENT: Industrial Relic Fragment
#### ID: exploration_cards_industrial_relic_fragment | Source: Base Game

1. If the relic deck is empty, a player may still purge three Industrial Relic Fragments as an action.
2. If an eliminated player had any Industrial Relic Fragments, they are discarded to the industrial planet exploration discard pile.
