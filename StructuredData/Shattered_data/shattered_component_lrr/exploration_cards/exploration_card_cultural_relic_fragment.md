# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Exploration Cards
## TYPE: Exploration_card
### COMPONENT: Cultural Relic Fragment
#### ID: exploration_cards_cultural_relic_fragment | Source: Base Game

1. If the relic deck is empty, a player may still purge three Cultural Relic Fragments as an action.
2. If an eliminated player had any Cultural Relic Fragments, they are discarded to the cultural planet exploration discard pile.
