# COMPONENT CLARIFICATION: Exploration Cards
## TYPE: Exploration_card
### COMPONENT: Unknown Relic Fragment
#### ID: exploration_cards_unknown_relic_fragment | Source: Base Game

1. A player cannot purge three Unknown Relic Fragments to gain a relic. At least one cultural, hazardous or industrial relic fragment must be purged.
2. If an eliminated player had any Unknown Relic Fragments, they are discarded to the frontier exploration discard pile.
