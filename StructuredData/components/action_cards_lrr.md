## Direct Hit
* **Component ID:** action_cards_direct_hit
* **Revision:** 0
* **Status:** Current
* **Source:** Base Game

### Clarification Notes
1. If multiple ships use theirSustain Damageability, aDirect Hitmay be played for each.
2. ADirect Hitmay be used whenever hits produced by a player’s units or abilities are cancelled by a ship using itsSustain Damageability.
3. When a player uses theSustain Damageability of one of their ships, they do not have to decide if another of their ships in the same combat will use itsSustain Damageability until after their opponent has used or declined to use aDirect Hit.
4. WhenDirect Hitis used outside of combat, if used on a ship with capacity, then fighters and/or ground forces must immediately be removed from the space area to meet the capacity limit.
5. A mech cannot be destroyed byDirect Hit, unless a game effect causes the mech to be considered a ship.
