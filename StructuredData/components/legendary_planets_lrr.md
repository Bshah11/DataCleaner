## Dok ’N Pic’s Salvage Yard — Garbozia
* **Component ID:** legendary_planets_dok_n_pics_salvage_yard_garbozia
* **Revision:** 0
* **Status:** Current
* **Source:** Base Game

### Clarification Notes
1. Cards onDok ’N Pic’s Salvage Yarddo not count towards a player’s action card hand limit.
2. The player withThe Codexcannot take cards that are onDok ’N Pic’s Salvage Yard.
3. If another player gains control of Garbozia, any action cards onDok ’N Pic’s Salvage Yardwill remain there when the legendary planet ability card is transferred; the new controller of Garbozia may play those action cards in the future.
4. Dok ’N Pic’s Salvage Yardneed not be readied for a player to play the action cards on it.

## The Galactic Council — Mecatol Rex
* **Component ID:** legendary_planets_the_galactic_council_mecatol_rex
* **Revision:** 0
* **Status:** Current
* **Source:** Base Game

### Clarification Notes
1. The Galactic Councilcannot be exhausted by a player that has no unscored secret objective cards in their hand.
2. When usingThe Galactic Council, the active player first discards a secret objective, shuffling it into the secret objective deck. Then, they draw a secret objective. This may result in them drawing the secret objective they discarded.
