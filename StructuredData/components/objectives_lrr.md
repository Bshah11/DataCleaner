## Become a Legend
* **Component ID:** objectives_become_a_legend
* **Revision:** 0
* **Status:** Current
* **Source:** Base Game

### Clarification Notes
1. The number of units in the system is irrelevant, if there is at least one.
2. A ship, ground force, or structure will all qualify forBecome a Legend.
3. If Mirage is placed in an anomaly, or theNano–Forgerelic is attached to a planet in an anomaly, that system will only count as one system towards the requirements ofBecome a Legend.
4. Any system containing a Vuil’raith player’s Dimensional Tear is a gravity rift, and thus, an anomaly.

## Become a Martyr
* **Component ID:** objectives_become_a_martyr
* **Revision:** 0
* **Status:** Current
* **Source:** Base Game

### Clarification Notes
1. The lost planet may be in any player’s home system. It does not have to be a planet in the home system of the player scoringBecome a Martyr.
2. If the lost planet is the last planet a player controls, that player will be eliminated before they scoreBecome a Martyr, if they meet the other requirements for elimination.
3. If the player with theShard of the Thronerelic loses their home system, they will lose theShard of the Thronecard and the associated victory point before they may scoreBecome a Martyr.
4. The attacker will have the opportunity to score a secret objective before the defender has the opportunity to scoreBecome a Martyr.

## Demonstrate Your Power

* **Revision:** 0
* **Status:** Current
* **Source:** Base Game

### Clarification Notes
1. A player need not win the combat to scoreDemonstrate Your Power; they are able to score it if their opponent plays theSkilled Retreataction card.
2. A player involved in a combat due to the Mahact player’sBenedictionhero ability may scoreDemonstrate Your Powerafter that combat.

4. The Mentak player may use theSleeper Cellability of their hero, during the last round of combat to place ships before scoringDemonstrate Your Power. However, fleet pool limits are also checked before scoring.

## Drive the Debate
* **Component ID:** objectives_drive_the_debate
* **Revision:** 0
* **Status:** Current
* **Source:** Base Game

### Clarification Notes
1. Any “For”/“Against” agenda cannot count forDrive the Debate, even if the outcome affects only a single player.
2. Drive the Debateis scored immediately before the agenda resolves. TheConfounding Legal TextandConfusing Legal Textaction cards will allow only the newly elected player to scoreDrive the Debate, and theDeadly Plotaction card will prevent any player from scoringDrive the Debate.
3. ScoringDrive the Debateimmediately before theColonial Redistributionagenda is resolved may change which player(s) can be chosen to place the infantry on the elected planet.
4. If a player drawsDrive the Debatewhile resolving theArchived SecretorSearch Warrantagendas, they cannot score it immediately.
